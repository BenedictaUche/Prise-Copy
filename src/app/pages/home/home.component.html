What is Prise?

Prise is an educational ERP designed to manage student finances using a smart identity card for payments within the school canteens and/or cafes, while also helping the school stay up-to-date with bills or tuition settlements from guardians or parents.

Why use Prise?

  • Increasing need for schools to implement and establish an entirely cashless system.
  • A lot of schools are dependent on periodic reconciliation of accounts.
  • Getting pocket money to students is also a challenging task for most parents.

Product Objectives

  • To promote cashless behaviour amongst students.
  • To allow parents top up students' pocket money easily without having to come to school.
  • To allow parents and school reconcile outstanding payments without hassle.
  • Allow students to inculcate saving habit

  • Access to remaining pocket money at the end of the term.
